Hangman
Rules SDP

RULES:

1. A random word will be listed using the dashes on the screen

2. Type a singular letter and click the button to enter

3. Keep entering letters to guess the word

4. You cannot guess the same letter twice!

5. If you guess the wrong letter, a body part will be added to the hangman

6. After 6 wrong guesses, the hangman will be complete and you lose!

How to win: Guess all the letters in the blank to complete the word and win!
